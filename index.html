About

The Artist

Israel Paketh is an internationally recognized tattoo artist specializing in geometric, blackwork, and ornamental tattooing. Working between Argentina and Koh Samui, Thailand, he creates bold, intricate designs rooted in sacred geometry, architectural patterns, and ornamental precision.


Known for large-scale body projects — full backs, sleeves, and bodysuits — his work balances mathematical precision with organic flow. Each piece is custom-designed, built to complement the body's natural lines and movement.


Israel works from @mindtripstudio in Koh Samui and travels internationally for conventions and guest spots. His signature style has earned a following of over 101,000 collectors and artists worldwide.
