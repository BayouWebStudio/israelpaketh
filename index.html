About the Artist

Precision.
Darkness. Flow.

Israel Paketh is a blackwork and ornamental tattoo artist known for bold geometric compositions, intricate linework, and deeply intentional designs. Every piece begins as a conversation — a collaboration that results in something truly one of a kind.

Specializing in blackwork, geometric, and ornamental styles, Israel creates tattoos that stand the test of time — both in technique and in meaning.
